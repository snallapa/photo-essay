What does music mean to you

What I was interested in finding out through this project was other people’s thoughts on music and how willing they would be to tell a random person. From the simple and open ended question “what does music mean to you,” I found 12 different answers of how music has affected people’s lives and found surprising amount of detail in their answers. I tried capturing the emotions throughout the short interview as they thought through the admittedly difficult opening question. Each person has the song they were currently listening to while the interview was happening added for your pleasure.

The End

I think it’s only just to end this with asking myself the question I uncomfortably (for both parties honestly) forced onto others. What does music mean to me? Music is a way of storytelling, something I love and feel is one of the strongest ways to connect with other people. It helps me push through hard times and brightens the good ones as well. Music can turn around my mood and give me the confidence get through rejection (like people saying no to being interviewed). It has helped me in almost every aspect of my life and at this point, I cannot imagine what I would be without it.
